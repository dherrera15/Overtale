Prepare for the underground!

Overtale is a Python-developed game that combines turn-based
combat mechanics with exploration and character progression.
You, as the player, take on the role of Storykeeper, whose
objective is to save the underground story from the Hollows,
and they're up to no good, they want to erase the history by
capturing the characters!
The player must build strategic teams and face the computer-
controlled enemies, and adapt to each battle to progress.
Thank you for trying out the game! This was developed using
the Tkinter library for the GUI, object-oriented programming
and recursion.
